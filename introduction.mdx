Reflect is **pure DeFi** - built from the Validator upwards. It is a suite of financial primitives that enable anyone to create and redeem *digital reflections of international currencies.* Reflect at its core is a synthetic RWA protocol; however, it's built with *native yield* in mind. Let us explore.
